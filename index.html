OVERVIEW

Each year ‘World Dream Day’ is celebrated on 25th September, a global initiative which encourages people to strive to make their dreams a reality. Dreamers are not only individuals or entrepreneurs who are starting up, but experienced leaders from across the industry, investors & others who are passionate about their vision and collectively partner to achieve results.
‘World Dream Day’ is a MIRAI JMAC Family Office global initiative aimed at helping the world reach its full potential. It advocates dreamers to move forward with their dreams, supports them with strategic mentorship & empowers their ideas with finance to ensure that they are successful. With this objective as the central theme it is our pursuit to host a global business summit bringing leaders, investors, venture capitalists and builders from Middle East, Africa, Japan and South East Asia together with a key focus to ‘Build Future Ready Business’.
The summit is endorsed by the private office of His Highness Sheikh Ahmed Bin Faisal Al-Qassimi in Middle East, Shah & Aruba family office in Africa and various global VC and family offices in the region. The Mirai JMAC Family office & Sangam Ventures will lead the conference by adding value via thought leadership. The summit will provide a platform to explore opportunities for collaborations, joint ventures and / or strategic tie-ups between large businesses, small & medium enterprises and start-up entrepreneurs within the MENA, Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Philippines, Vietnam, Japan and India.
We propose to bring regional & global transformational leaders together, representation via a delegation of senior leaders from business enterprises, start-ups and SMEs will comprise the ‘Business Vertical’. To compliment this the summit will provide a robust platform to new & existing technology solution providers to share solutions as part of the ‘Technical Vertical’. The summit will also provide a networking opportunity to leaders from UAE, India, Singapore, Vietnam, Japan & beyond who will comprise the ‘People Vertical’.
